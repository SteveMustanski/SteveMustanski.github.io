Agilist | Full-Stack Developer | Teacher

Agilist

I have over 10 years experience coaching teams in agile software delivery utilizing scrum, lean agile, and kanban delivery methods.

By focusing on incrementally delivering their customer's highest priority features, the teams I coach are able to deliver quickly, without compromising quality. I strive to work with teams and their product owners to ensure that teams are reliably delivering continuous value to their customers.

I coach teams to constantly look at their software delivery process and discover opportunities to improve how they deliver working software.

I also coach teams to adopt test automation, pairing, and devops to improve their delivery capabilities.

Full-Stack Developer

As an agile delivery lead with several years of software development experience, I continulally refresh my technical skills so that in addition to coaching teams, I also develop software and perform Dev Ops duties.

My current team is replacing a legacy application with an in-house developed solution utilizing Vue, Node, and PostgreSQL. We will soon be developing to a Micro UI architecture adding Angular to the mix of frameworks our application is build on.

In order to broaden my technical expertise, I became an AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate in 2017 and have re-certified in 2020.

Teacher

I work part-time at the University of Richmond as an instructor and teaching assistant for a six month full-stack web development bootcamp. The bootcamp is delivered in collaboration with Trilogy Education Services.

Students learn HTML/CSS, JavaScript, database, NodeJS, and React during the bootcamp. Students also learn Git, termimal, and other full-stack web development tools and techiques. In the final weeks of the bootcamp, students build and deploy a full-stack application to the cloud.

I deliver lectures, demonstrate code examples, and assist students with in-class activities, troubleshooting homework, and assist with projects.
